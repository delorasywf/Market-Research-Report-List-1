<p><p>The retro-reflective photoelectric sensor market is divided into two main types: separate and compact sensors. Separate sensors consist of a transmitter and a receiver placed apart, allowing for longer detection ranges and flexibility in installation. In contrast, compact sensors integrate both transmitter and receiver into a single unit, making them ideal for limited spaces and simplifying installation. Both types are widely used in industrial automation, material handling, and safety applications, catering to various operational requirements and preferences.</p></p>
