<p><p>The sweeteners for food and beverage market comprises several types, including oligosaccharides, inulin, sugar alcohols, and others. Sugar alcohols, like xylitol and erythritol, are low-calorie sweeteners that can mimic sugar's taste without the same caloric impact. Other categories include natural and artificial sweeteners, catering to diverse consumer preferences for health and diet.</p></p>
