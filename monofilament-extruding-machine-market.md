PLC linkage machines integrate programmable logic controllers for centralized automation and control, offering enhanced efficiency and precision in production. They are suited for complex manufacturing environments. In contrast, stand-alone control machines operate independently, allowing for straightforward setup and operation without the need for intricate programming. This type is ideal for smaller operations or less complex applications, providing flexibility and ease of use.</p></p>
