<p><ul><li>Water-Base</li><li>Silicone-Base</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The face primer market can be categorized into two main types: water-based and silicone-based primers. Water-based primers are lightweight and hydrating, making them ideal for those with oily or combination skin; they create a breathable base that helps makeup adhere while providing moisture. In contrast, silicone-based primers offer a smooth, velvety finish, filling in fine lines and pores for a flawless look. This type is often favored for its matte finish, particularly by individuals with normal to dry skin types.</p></p>
