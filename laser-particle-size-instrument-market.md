<p><p>The laser particle size instrument market is segmented into dry test and wet test types, each catering to different applications. Dry test methods utilize air or gas to analyze particles in powdered samples, making them suitable for industries like pharmaceuticals and construction. In contrast, wet test methods involve suspending particles in a liquid medium, offering enhanced accuracy for fine particles and used primarily in the chemical and food industries. Both methods leverage laser diffraction for precise particle size measurement.</p></p>
