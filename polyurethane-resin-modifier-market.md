<p><ul><li>Monofunctional Modifier</li><li>Multifunctional Modifier</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyurethane resin modifier market is categorized into two main types: monofunctional and multifunctional modifiers. Monofunctional modifiers typically possess a single reactive site, enhancing specific properties like flexibility or adhesion in polyurethane systems. In contrast, multifunctional modifiers contain multiple reactive sites, allowing for improved crosslinking and enhanced performance characteristics such as durability and chemical resistance. Both types are crucial in tailoring polyurethane properties for diverse applications, including coatings, adhesives, foams, and sealants, to meet various industrial requirements.</p></p>
