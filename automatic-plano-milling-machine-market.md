<p><p>The automatic plano milling machine market is segmented into two primary types: horizontal milling machines and vertical milling machines. Horizontal milling machines operate with the cutting tool parallel to the workpiece, allowing for efficient material removal and complex contours. In contrast, vertical milling machines position the cutter perpendicular to the workpiece, providing versatility for detailed operations such as drilling and shaping. Both types cater to different manufacturing needs, enhancing productivity and precision in various industrial applications.</p></p>
