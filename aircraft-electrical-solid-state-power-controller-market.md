In contrast, three-phase controllers handle larger loads and are essential for high-performance systems, offering better efficiency and reliability. Both types provide important functionalities such as fault detection, power monitoring, and remote control, contributing to improved safety and operational efficiency in modern aircraft electrical systems.</p></p>
