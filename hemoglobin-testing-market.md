<p><strong>The Hemoglobin Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Chromatography</li><li>Immunoassay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hemoglobin testing market includes various methods like chromatography and immunoassays. Chromatography separates different hemoglobin types, providing accurate quantification and analysis of variants, which is crucial for diagnosing disorders like sickle cell disease and thalassemia. Immunoassays, on the other hand, utilize antibodies to detect and measure hemoglobin levels, allowing for rapid results and ease of use in clinical settings. Both methods play a vital role in facilitating effective patient management and monitoring hemoglobin-related conditions.</p></p>
