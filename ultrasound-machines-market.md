<p><strong>The Ultrasound Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>A-Mode</li><li>B-Mode or 2D Mode</li><li>C-Mode</li><li>M-Mode</li><li>Doppler Mode</li><li>Pulse Inversion Mode</li><li>Harmonic Mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ultrasound machines operate through various modes catering to different diagnostic needs. A-Mode provides a simple amplitude display for measuring distances. B-Mode (2D Mode) offers grayscale images for visualizing structures. C-Mode is utilized for cross-sectional imaging. M-Mode captures motion over time, ideal for cardiac assessments. Doppler Mode evaluates blood flow and velocity. Pulse Inversion Mode enhances image quality by reducing artifacts, while Harmonic Mode improves resolution and contrast. Together, these modes support diverse clinical applications and enhance diagnostic accuracy.</p></p>
