<p><p>The Constant Voltage Transformers (CVTs) market is divided into two primary types: single-phase and three-phase transformers. Single-phase CVTs are typically used in residential and small commercial applications, ensuring a stable voltage supply for devices. In contrast, three-phase CVTs cater to industrial settings, managing higher loads and enhancing efficiency by supplying balanced voltage across three electrical phases. Both types aim to protect equipment from voltage fluctuations, improving reliability, and extending the lifespan of connected electrical devices.</p></p>
